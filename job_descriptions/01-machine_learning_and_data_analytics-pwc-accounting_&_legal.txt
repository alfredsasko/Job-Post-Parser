Job description: 

- Presenting results of research to the client 

- As a machine learning consultant you will work closely with clients to gather the undertstanding of their business goals and create a proper problem statement. Once it is confirmed that solving particular problem helps the client to achieve business goal and it is a case for ML, the work starts. You work with the client to collect information and data needed to create a model, perform the research and iteratively prepare the model. By the time the model is tested you need to learn the client infrastructure and support the roll out the product following client's constraints. Experienced consultants will support you during your work, however, it is expected that soon you learn enough to understand goals, build models, wrap them into Restful API-s, place them into Docker containers and guide clients through the process of implementation.
